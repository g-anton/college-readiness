College Readiness

Are you a freshman in highschool who is starting to think about college? A student who feels scared to dive into the "adult" world. A student who needs a bit of guidance well this is checklist for you! You will be provided with tips/tricks to ease the college experience. At the end you will be to download a PNG version of the checklist to keep with you through your college prep journey.

Start by speaking with your advisor about the possibility of taking accelerated math courses. Studies have shown students who partake in higher level, rigorous courses are more likely to further their academic succes in college. Consider taking Alegbra 1 to then move onto to a calculus track to prepare.

After speaking with your advisor set up a meeting with a tutor. This will be benefical if you find the coursework to be a bit of a challenge. A tutor will assit you not only with assignments but provide you with additional tools for overall structure. This is a great resource to prepare for college!
